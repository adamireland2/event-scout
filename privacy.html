Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 20, 2026

Overview

Event Scout is a tool that helps professionals find relevant industry events and conferences. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

What data does Event Scout collect?

Event Scout does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. Specifically:

  • API Key: Your Anthropic API key is stored only in your device's local browser storage. It is never sent to any server other than Anthropic's API (api.anthropic.com) to process your search requests.
  • Search Queries: Your event search queries are sent directly from your device to Anthropic's API. We do not see, log, or store these queries.
  • Search Results: Event results are returned directly from Anthropic's API to your device. We do not intercept or store them.
  • Location: City selections are used only to formulate your search query. No GPS or device location data is collected.

Third-party services

Event Scout uses the following third-party service:

  • Anthropic API: Your search queries are processed by Anthropic's Claude AI. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to data processed through their API. See: anthropic.com/privacy

Data storage

The only data stored on your device is your Anthropic API key (in the browser's localStorage). You can delete this at any time by clearing your browser data or clicking "Settings" within the app.

Children's privacy

Event Scout is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
